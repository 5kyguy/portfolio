### pptGEN

- **Links**: [Website](https://synslidegen.github.io/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/synslidegen) | [Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.23605) | [Early Dev (My contribution)](https://github.com/NerdyVisky/pptGEN-dev)
- **Period**: Jan 2024 - Jun 2024
- **Image**: /assets/projects/pptgen.png
- **Stack**: Python, PyTorch, Transformers, Hugging Face, LangChain, OpenAI API
- **Description**: A pipeline to generate synthetic data on educational slides and PPT content, for training document understanding models. Slides are among the most unstructured documents to train models on, with limited datasets available. pptGEN takes in course lists and syllabi and creates realistic PPTs with randomness in style, font, and design.
- **Highlights**: Co-built the synthetic slide generation pipeline for document understanding model training, at initial stages of the project. Could not make it to the final paper as I had started my full time job at Lampros Tech Labs, but learned a lot about the research process and the importance of reproducibility.
