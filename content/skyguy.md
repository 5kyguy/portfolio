### pptGEN

- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NerdyVisky/pptGEN-dev | https://github.com/synslidegen/pptGEN
- **Image**: /assets/projects/p3.jpg
- **Stack**: Python, PyTorch, Transformers, Hugging Face, LangChain, OpenAI API

A pipeline to generate synthetic data on educational slides and PPT content, for training document understanding models. Slides are among the most unstructured documents to train models on, with limited datasets available. pptGEN takes in course lists and syllabi and creates realistic PPTs with randomness in style, font, and design.
